Our Goal

To empower our customers with a skilled, creative, and dedicated team capable of delivering exceptional supply services and consultation, overcoming any geographical or environmental challenges.

We are committed to maintaining an ethical environment with high business standards, fostering growth, innovation, and consistent profitability to meet the expectations of our customers and the wider business community

Our Commitment

We are committed to optimizing procurement and supply services through effective demand and supply chain management. This ensures that customers receive precisely the products and services specified in their orders, delivered with accuracy and reliability.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of exceptional procurement and supply services in Tanzania, recognized for our reliability, innovation, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We aim to set the benchmark for ethical business practices, fostering sustainable growth and contributing to the development of the communities we serve.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality products at competitive and fair prices, ensuring timely service while maintaining a sustainable profit margin. We are committed to providing reliable, professional, and effective solutions, supported by our skilled staff, shareholders, business partners, and the wider public across the nation. Our operations adhere to industry standards, emphasizing excellence, punctuality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Services

Najuna General Supplies, we pride ourselves on delivering a diverse range of services designed to meet the dynamic needs of our clients. Our offerings include:

Fumigation and Fire Safety Solutions

We offer professional fumigation services to ensure pest-free environments, along with the installation and maintenance of fire fighting equipment, including fire extinguishers, to enhance safety and compliance in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Printing Services

We provide high-quality printing solutions for all your needs, including promotional materials like brochures, flyers, and banners, as well as custom stationery and branded items. Our services ensure precision, creativity, and timely delivery to make your ideas stand out.

Supply of Uniforms

We specialize in the supply of high-quality uniforms for various sectors, including corporate, industrial, healthcare, education, hospitality, and security. Our uniforms are designed for comfort, durability, and professional appearance, tailored to meet the specific needs of each sector.

Supply of Stationery and Office Equipment

We provide a wide range of stationery and office supplies, including paper, pens, folders, and other essential items, along with office equipment such as printers, copiers, and computers to keep your workspace fully equipped and efficient.

Cleaning Services and Garden Maintenance
